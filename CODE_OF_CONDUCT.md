# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for all
contributors, maintaining the highest standards of mathematical and academic integrity.

## Standards

- Cite all prior mathematical work accurately
- Distinguish between established results and novel contributions
- Use precise mathematical notation
- Acknowledge when propositions require further validation
